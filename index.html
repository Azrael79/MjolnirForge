Account & Access What to know about app access, local data, and missing paid access. Learn more

No separate Mjölnir account

  • Mjölnir does not currently use a separate Mjölnir username or password.
  • Your athlete profile, programs, and workout logs are stored on your device.
  • If you delete the app or move to a new device, local app data may not appear unless it was restored by your device backup.

If paid access is not showing

  1. Make sure your device is connected to the internet.
  2. Confirm you are signed in with the Apple ID that purchased the subscription.
  3. In Mjölnir, tap Restore Purchases if you see the subscription screen.
  4. Or open Profile, go to Subscription, and tap Refresh App Store Entitlement.
  5. Close and reopen Mjölnir if the App Store says your subscription is active but the app has not refreshed yet.

If you contact support, include what you tapped, what message you saw, your iPhone model, and your iOS version.

Training & Programs How to set up a plan, adjust it, and keep your training log useful. Learn more

Before you create a program

  • Fill out your athlete profile as accurately as you can.
  • Check your training days, equipment, weight unit, body weight, injuries, and training maxes.
  • If you are training for a show, enter the competition events and target weights before creating the plan.
  • Use current, realistic maxes. The plan is more useful when it starts from where you are now.

If the plan looks wrong

  • Go back to your profile and check the details that feed the plan.
  • Update the information that changed, such as equipment, training days, maxes, injuries, or event selections.
  • Create the program again after those details are corrected.

Logging workouts

  • Log the weight, reps, effort, and notes that matter for the session.
  • If you substitute an exercise, choose something that trains the same basic movement or event when possible.
  • Use your own judgment and coaching guidance when choosing loads, especially on days you feel unusually tired or beat up.

Mjölnir is a planning tool. It does not replace a coach, clinician, or your own judgment during training.

Pregnancy & Postpartum How to use pregnancy and postpartum settings without treating the app as medical advice. Learn more

Use the right mode

  • Use pregnancy settings if you are currently pregnant.
  • Use postpartum settings if you are returning to training after delivery.
  • Keep your week of pregnancy, due date, delivery date, delivery type, and clearance status up to date.

Before training

  • Follow guidance from your doctor, midwife, pelvic health physical therapist, or other qualified clinician.
  • Answer check-in questions honestly. The app can only respond to the information you enter.
  • If your symptoms or clearance status changes, update Mjölnir before using the plan again.

When to stop

  • Stop training and seek appropriate care for warning symptoms such as bleeding, dizziness, chest pain, severe pelvic pain, fluid leakage, severe headache, vision changes, or unusual shortness of breath.
  • Postpartum, also stop for worsening incision or wound symptoms, pelvic heaviness or bulging, leaking, or pain that increases during or after training.

Pregnancy and postpartum guidance in Mjölnir is educational and conservative. It is not medical care, diagnosis, or treatment.

Reports & Export How to export a training report, program PDF, or program JSON file. Learn more

Choose the right export

  • Training Report (PDF) creates a coach-friendly summary from your saved program and logged workouts.
  • Program PDF creates a readable copy of the program plan.
  • Program JSON creates a structured data file. Most users will want one of the PDF options.

Export a report or program

  1. Open Mjölnir and go to your saved program.
  2. Tap the share icon, which looks like a square with an arrow pointing up.
  3. Choose Training Report (PDF), Program PDF, or Program JSON.
  4. When Mjölnir says the export is ready, tap Share PDF or Share JSON.
  5. Choose where to send or save the file, such as Files, Mail, Messages, or AirDrop.

If export is not available

  • Make sure the program is saved, not just previewed.
  • Training Report (PDF) works best after you have logged workouts in the program.
  • Export may require Full Monthly access. If you subscribed but export is still locked, use Restore Purchases or Refresh App Store Entitlement.

If an export fails, close and reopen Mjölnir, try again, and contact support with the message you saw.

Billing & Subscriptions How to cancel, restore access, or request a refund through Apple. Learn more

Cancel future billing

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  2. Tap your name at the top.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Tap Mjölnir.
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription. If there is no cancel button, Apple may already show it as canceled or expired.

Restore access in Mjölnir

  • Use Restore Purchases in Mjölnir after reinstalling the app or switching devices.
  • If you can open the Profile tab, go to Subscription and tap Refresh App Store Entitlement.
  • Make sure you are using the Apple ID that bought the subscription.

Request a refund from Apple

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with the Apple ID that was charged.
  2. Choose I'd like to, then choose Request a refund.
  3. Choose the reason for the request, then tap Next.
  4. Select Mjölnir or the Mjölnir subscription, then tap Submit.

Apple decides refund eligibility. Refunds can vary by country or region, and pending charges may not be available for a refund request until Apple sends a receipt. A refund request does not always cancel future billing, so cancel the subscription separately if you do not want it to renew.
