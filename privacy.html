Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Mjölnir stores, what is sent off your device, how support requests are handled, and how you can delete your data.

Effective date: June 14, 2026 Contact: support@mjolnir.app

Summary

Mjölnir is designed as a local-first training planner. The app stores your training profile, generated programs, workout logs, pregnancy and postpartum settings, and symptom check-ins on your device. Mjölnir does not operate a backend service in its current release, does not sell personal information, does not use third-party advertising SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

If you email support, the information you choose to include in that message is received and used to respond to you.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app may store the following information locally so it can generate and manage training plans:

  • Profile details, such as name, training track, experience level, training days, preferred weight unit, age, gender, body weight, injuries, available equipment, and one-rep maxes.
  • Competition and program details, such as event selections, target loads, program weeks, exercises, sets, reps, training notes, substitutions, stress notes, and progression information.
  • Workout log details, such as completed workouts, completed exercises, set weight, reps, RPE, distance, duration, notes, and timestamps.
  • Pregnancy and postpartum mode details, such as pregnancy week or due date, delivery date or delivery type, lactation status, clinician clearance status, contraindication screening selections, consent acknowledgment, training preferences, and return-to-activity checklist status.
  • Pregnancy and postpartum check-ins, such as energy, nausea, fatigue, sleep quality, pelvic floor symptoms, core symptoms, pain symptoms, red-flag symptoms, postpartum wound symptoms, notes, and timestamps.
  • Subscription status details available through StoreKit, such as product ID, transaction identifiers, purchase date, expiration date, revocation date, environment, and verification timestamp.

This information is stored on your device using Apple's local storage technologies. It is not sent to Mjölnir unless you choose to share it, for example by emailing support.

Information Mjölnir Collects from the App

Mjölnir does not currently collect app usage analytics, training data, pregnancy or postpartum data, workout logs, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, location data, contacts, photos, or HealthKit data on a Mjölnir server.

The app's privacy manifest declares no tracking, no tracking domains, no collected data types, and no accessed required-reason API categories for the app's own code.

Purchases and Subscriptions

Mjölnir may offer an auto-renewable monthly subscription through the App Store. Purchases, renewals, cancellations, refunds, billing history, payment details, and payment method information are handled by Apple. Mjölnir uses StoreKit to determine whether full access should be unlocked on your device.

We do not receive your full payment card number or Apple ID password.

Support Requests

When you contact support by email, we receive the email address you use, your message, and any screenshots, logs, app details, health details, pregnancy or postpartum details, training details, or other information you choose to include. We use support emails only to investigate, respond to, and resolve your request.

Please avoid sending sensitive health information unless it is necessary for your support question. Mjölnir support is not emergency support and does not provide medical advice.

Sharing

Mjölnir does not sell personal information. We may share information only in the following limited situations:

  • With Apple, where Apple independently processes App Store purchases, subscriptions, refunds, and related account services.
  • With email or support service providers that process support messages on our behalf.
  • When required to comply with law, protect rights, prevent fraud, or respond to lawful requests.

Retention and Deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, reset the app's data if that feature is available, or delete the app from your device. You can also contact support for help understanding what local data the app stores.

Support emails are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to resolve support requests, maintain support records, and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of support emails by contacting support@mjolnir.app.

Your Choices and Rights

You can choose what information to enter into the app. You can avoid sending personal information to support by limiting what you include in emails. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of personal information held about you. Because Mjölnir stores app data locally, many requests can be completed directly on your device.

Children

Mjölnir is not intended for children under 13. The pregnancy and postpartum features are intended for users who can make their own health and training decisions or who are using the app with appropriate adult, coach, or clinician guidance.

Health and Safety Notice

Mjölnir provides training planning and educational information. It is not a medical device, medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency service. Pregnancy, postpartum recovery, injuries, and heavy training can involve real risk. Consult a qualified clinician before beginning or changing training, and stop training if warning symptoms occur.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Mjölnir changes. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page. Material privacy changes should be reviewed before continuing to use the app.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact support@mjolnir.app.
